Hi, I'm Ryan.

Who I am

Brown belt in jiu jitsu. Dad with a multitude of joint issues. Beer connoisseur. Tech fiend.

I've been using LLMs since they were shitty. I'm a non-technical rage builder. I once tried to learn to code by building a side-scroller game in Python. The main character was a squirrel.

I switch up direction a lot. Honestly it's my superpower. A single look at my non-existent resume would turn a niche-maxer to stone.

Here's what I know for certain

From all the people I've worked for and all the businesses I've frequented — they truly do not care about your CRM with 5000 features and an AI assistant.

They care about time, resources, ease of use. They operate on one problem at a time.

When you explain your workflow to them their eyes glaze over. Not because they don't care — because they've got six other fires to put out before lunch.

The learning curve nobody talks about

That's not to say SaaS isn't helpful to small businesses.

But if there's a learning curve, you have to understand something.

They just don't have the time.

The moment that changed everything

The other day my mom was spending hours writing quotes for her catering business and asked me to help her.

She had a calculator. She was copy-pasting old quotes and filling out the new numbers. Not even a hot key was used.

I wasn't going to sit there and do the same thing. So what did I do?

I popped open Claude Code and spun up a quote generator — all her menu items, pricing, and service charges. Ran locally on her desktop for her to always have access to.

Saved her 5 hours of time. Made me look like I performed voodoo magic.

No subscription. No login. Not even an internet connection. Simplicity at its finest.

I've been solving the wrong problems

It took me weeks to realize it.

I'll let the tech bros build web3 apps, agentic web scrapers, and the next vybe coding IDE.

I want to solve problems for the people who don't have the time to set up Open WebUI and build their personal AI assistant.

Why I'm building Terrapin

A simple web app built for people who work.

A library of tools that save hours on unsexy tasks.

Built for humans first. No login. No expensive subscription. You can run almost all of the tools locally on your computer.

Caterers, dry cleaners, coffee shops, street vendors, bars, restaurants, farmers market purveyors — I'm here to build for you.

You are the destinations that make a day a day and a night a night.

AI can work for you

It doesn't have to be complicated to make an impact on your business.

First we start with you. And when the time is right I'll let the agents through the door.

But first we start by saving you time. 🐢
