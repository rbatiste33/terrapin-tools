Questions

The stuff people ask before installing

What happens if I install and don't like it?
Uninstall is one line in Terminal. Every file Terrapin creates lives in the folder ~/.terrapin/ on your Mac. Delete that folder and everything is gone — profile, receipts, CRM, everything. You're not locked in.
Is it really free?
Every core tool is free forever. No account, no subscription, no trial. Some tools are premium — one-time purchase ($29 today, may vary per tool). You buy once and own it forever. No recurring charges, ever.
Do I need to be technical?
You need to paste one line into Terminal to install. After that, everything is point-and-click in your browser. If you got through "install the App Store," you can handle this.
What if Terrapin shuts down or goes out of business?
Your data doesn't depend on us. It's in open JSON and text files on your Mac. You can open them in any text editor. If we disappear tomorrow, your business data is still yours and still readable forever.
Does any of my business data leave my computer?
No. The AI runs on your Mac via Ollama. Your receipts, invoices, CRM, calendar — all local. The only exceptions: sending email goes through your own Gmail account, and clicking the "Did this help?" feedback button on any tool sends your message to Ryan. Both require your explicit action.
Why do I need to install something? Can't it just run in the browser?
Most tools already run in the browser alone — no install needed. Install is only required for the local AI (Gemma 4 via Ollama) and for persistent storage of your data. If you want an AI that never sees the cloud, you need to run it on your machine.
What's the difference between free and premium tools?
Free tools do structured work — invoices, CRM, calendar, QR codes, order logs. Premium tools use AI heavily and save you real time. Smart Receipt Box (our first premium) reads a photo of a receipt and extracts every detail in 10 seconds. That's worth $29 once.
Windows or Linux?
Mac only for now. Windows installer is in the queue. Linux is on the list after that.
Where does my payment go if I buy a premium tool?
Through Gumroad, a trusted indie-software payment processor. Gumroad emails you a license key. You paste it into the tool. Done. We don't handle your card details directly. Refunds are available through Gumroad.
What if I have a question or something breaks?
Email support@terrapin.tools — I (Ryan) read every message and reply personally. You can also use the "Did this help?" button at the bottom of any tool.
