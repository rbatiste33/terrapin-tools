Before you install

Real questions. Straight answers.

Do I need to be a developer?
No. You paste one line into Terminal once. That's it. If you've ever installed something from the Mac App Store, you can handle this. After setup, everything is point-and-click.
What if I install it and hate it?
Delete one folder. ~/.terrapin/ holds everything — profile, receipts, client list, logs. Remove it and Terrapin never existed on your machine. You're not locked in.
Does my business data leave my computer?
No. The AI runs locally on your Mac through Ollama. Your invoices, receipts, and CRM never touch a server we control. The only exceptions: sending email goes through your own Gmail, and clicking a "was this useful?" feedback button sends a message to Ryan. Both require your explicit action.
What if Terrapin shuts down next year?
Your data doesn't depend on us. Everything is plain JSON and text files on your Mac. If we disappear tomorrow, your invoices, receipts, and client list are still yours, still readable, still usable in any other tool.
Why do I need to install anything?
Most tools already work in the browser — zero install. You only need the install if you want the local AI agent (Gemma 4 via Ollama) and permanent storage on your machine. That's what makes it genuinely private.
Windows or Linux?
Mac-only for now. Windows installer is in the queue. Linux after that. The browser tools work everywhere.
What does the premium tool get me?
Smart Receipt Box is $29 once — you pay once, own it forever. It uses local Gemma 4 vision to read receipts, extract vendor / date / line items / totals, and auto-categorize. Works offline. Never uploads your receipts anywhere.

Paste one command. Walk away for 10 minutes.

Everything installs itself: Ollama, Gemma 4, the agent. Nothing asks for a credit card. Nothing phones home. Delete the folder if you change your mind.
