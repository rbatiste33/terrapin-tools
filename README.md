# Terrapin Tools

Simple tools for people who work. No login. No subscription. No cloud. Just tools that do one job and do it well.

Built for the coffee shop, the dry cleaner, the contractor, the taco stand, the catering company, the freelancer.

## What this is
A library of locally-run tools for small businesses. Every tool:
- Runs in your browser — no install required
- Saves data on your machine — nothing goes to a server
- Works offline — no internet needed
- Is free to use at terrapin.tools

## Privacy
Every tool runs locally on your computer. We never see your data. We never store your data. We never connect your business information to a cloud AI model. Read our full privacy promise at terrapin.tools/privacy

## Sponsoring a tool
Local businesses can sponsor a tool build. Your logo lives on it permanently. No ads, no algorithms, just honest community support. See terrapin.tools/sponsor
